## Configuration Information

1. Go to the [COS Console](https://curl.qcloud.com/tPoI2o1J) to create a bucket.

2. Fill in the `Region` with the English part inside the parentheses.
3. Fill in the `Bucket` with the bucket name.
4. Fill in the `Endpoint` with the access domain name, removing the initial bucket name.
 ![](../../../zh-CN/guide/storage/cos/1.webp)

5. Go to the [Access Key - Console](https://curl.qcloud.com/svYftakQ) to get the keys. Fill in the `SecretId` into `Access Key` and the `SecretKey` into `Secret Key`.
