## Configuration Information

1. Go to the [COS Console](https://curl.qcloud.com/tPoI2o1J) to create a bucket.
2. Fill in the `Region` with the English part in the brackets.
3. Fill in the `Bucket` with the bucket name.
4. Fill in the `Endpoint` with the access domain name, removing the bucket name at the beginning.
 ![](./cos/1.webp)

5. Go to the [Access Key - Console](https://curl.qcloud.com/svYftakQ) to get the keys, enter the `SecretId` in the `Access Key` field, and enter the `SecretKey` in the `Secret Key` field.

5. 前往 [访问密钥 - 控制台](https://curl.qcloud.com/svYftakQ) 获取密钥，将`SecretId`填入`Access Key`，将`SecretKey`填入`Secret Key`
